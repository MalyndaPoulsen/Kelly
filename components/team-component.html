Kelly Young

Kelly Young is a registered nurse for both the state of Idaho and Oregon. She recieved her B.S. in Nursing from Boise State University and also recieved a B.S. in Exercise and Sport Science from Oregon State University. She is also a fitness provider who has spent many years teaching fitness classes and providing personal training. Currently she manages a home care business and teaches yoga.

"Everyday I see the importance of providing excellent care for both our aging loved ones and those who care for them daily." -Kelly Young
